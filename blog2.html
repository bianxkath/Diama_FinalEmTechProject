How do we draw a line between "content" and harmful media (e.g. hate speech, misinformation, explicit content, etc.)?

We can not deny that nowadays, the media is a very powerful tool to the point that it can cause harm to us, users. We are constantly exposed on social media in this digital age. We can now easily use open platforms to post or share our ideas, thoughts, and even personal experiences with others. Unfortunately, this can result in harmful media such as hate speech, misinformation, explicit content, and other issues. As a result, it is critical to understand how we distinguish between "content" and harmful media.
